01About

I'm an electrical engineering undergrad currently at Wilbur Wright College, transferring to the University of Illinois Chicago (UIC) to continue my degree. I'm drawn to problems that span the full stack of hardware — from the physics of a device up through the control systems and algorithms running on top of it.

Lately that's meant researching quantum simulation algorithms through an NSF-funded summer program, and building small embedded robotics projects on the side. I'm especially interested in where quantum computing, embedded systems, and semiconductor devices intersect — and in finding a grad program where I can dig into that further.
